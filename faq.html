Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the creation, care, and ordering of handcrafted permanent wood art.

Our pricing is meticulously calculated based on three primary factors: dimension, complexity of detail (portraits involve intense focus compared to patterns), and the scarcity of the hardwood. We provide a comprehensive quote before any burning begins.

Because pyrography is an unforgiving, slow, and deliberate craft, most custom pieces require 3 to 5 weeks from finalized design approval. Exceptionally large or intricately shaded portraits may require additional time.

Our works are finished with museum-grade, UV-resistant sealants. To ensure multi-generational longevity, avoid placing the piece in direct punishing sunlight and maintain a stable indoor environment. Dust gently with a clean, dry microfiber cloth.

Absolutely. High-resolution, clear photography is ideal for translating emotion to wood. During our consultation, we’ll discuss how the specific grain of the wood will interact with your unique photograph.

Yes, we ship our handcrafted masterworks worldwide. Every international order is meticulously packed, fully insured, and tracked to allow you to monitor its journey to your doorstep.
