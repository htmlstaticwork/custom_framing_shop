The Artisan Conservator

Meet the master framer dedicated to the preservation of art, memorabilia, and heritage through artisanal curation.

A Journey in Curation

I believe every piece of art has a soul, and as a conservator, my role is to create the sanctuary that protects it.

My journey began in a prestigious art gallery in California, where I first discovered the profound impact that the right frame can have on a piece's longevity and presence. What started as a study of materials quickly became a passion for creating bespoke preservation solutions.

There is a deep satisfaction in selecting the perfect silk mat or hand-joining a walnut frame that will house a family legacy. Today, I specialize in museum-quality custom framing, blending structural preservation with a signature artisanal touch.

Why Choose the Artisan Path?

  • Authentic Artistry

    No lasers or AI etching. Every line is hand-burned, ensuring an authentic connection to the artist's hand and soul.

  • Archival Standards

    We use only high-grade hardwoods and pH-neutral museum-quality sealants to ensure your artwork lasts for generations.

  • Collaborative Design

The Heritage of Pyrography

Dating back centuries, the art of burning designs into wood—pyrography—was a way for cultures to preserve history and tell stories.

At Artisan Woodworks, we don't just use fire; we honor a legacy. By blending ancient techniques with museum-grade archival materials, we ensure that the stories we etch today will endure for generations, just like the artifacts that inspired us.
